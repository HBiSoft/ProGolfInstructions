Students


Adding a student


Press the blue plus sign



Then press "Add Student"

a New page will open. Here you can provide your students name, age, handicap and you can select a profile image if you want.



When you are done, press "ADD".
Your student profile will now be visible in the "Students" tab.


Student Profile




Here is where all your students videos, images and lessons will be saved to.

To add a video, lesson or to edit your students information, press the blue plus sign.


Student Lessons


You can add a student lesson by pressing the blue plus sign




After filling in all the fields, press the "Save Lesson" button.

The lesson will now be visible in the "Lessons" tab in your students profile page.

When you tap on the lesson, the following page will open.



You can edit all the fields on this page by tapping on them.

You can also add videos and images to the lesson by tapping on the blue plus sign.

All the selected videos and images will be sent to the student, as attachments, when you press the share button.
