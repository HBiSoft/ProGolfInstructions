Drawing
Tools


Line

The line tool has 3 anchor points.

One in the center of the line and one on each side of the line. The anchor points on the side are used to resize the line and the one in the center is to move the line around.

These anchor points can be made visible in the Settings tab.



Circle

The circular tool has 2 anchor points.

One in the center to move the circle around and one in the bottom right corner to resize the circle.



Rectangle

The rectangular tool has 2 anchor points.

One in the center to move the rectangle around and one in the bottom right corner to resize the rectangle.



Arrow

The arrow tool has 3 anchor points.

One in the center of the arrow and one on each side of the arrow. The anchor points on the side is to resize the arrow and the one in the center is to move the arrow around.



Angle

The angular tool has 3 anchor points.

One in the center and one on the end of each line. The anchors on the end are used to adjust the angle and the one in the center is to move the angle around.



Freehand Tool

The freehand tool doesn't have adjustable anchor points.

When exporting a video, lines/shapes that was drawn with the freehand tool, will not be included in the exported video.
